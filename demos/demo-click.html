Click Demo - Clickable, Hoverable, Focusable, Selectable

Demo Information

Behaviors demonstrated:

  • Hoverable: Buttons highlight on hover
  • Focusable: Press T to focus items, arrow keys to navigate
  • Clickable: Single click, double click, long press events
  • Selectable: Toggle selection with click
  • Disableable: Disable checkbox prevents interaction

Try this:

  • Click the buttons and watch for click events
  • Press and hold for long press (0.6s)
  • Double-click quickly for double click event
  • Press T to focus items, use arrow keys to move focus
  • Click toggles to select items
  • Check the disable box to disable the red button
